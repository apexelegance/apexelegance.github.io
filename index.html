Our Work

Hello everyone, we are the members of the F1 in schools, our team name is Apex Elegance.

F1 in school, according to the name, it actually mean Formula 1 in Schools. We need to make a 3d model to evolve a f1 car, utilize CNC technique and 3D print skill to produce the whole car. We need to write portfolio, engineer drawing, and rendering to propaganda our team.

CAD

Autodesk Fusion

We used Autodesk Fusion to design our car and create a 3D model based on our learnt skills.

Analysis

Ansys Discovery

In the middle lesson, we had learnt how to use Ansys Discovery, which can simulate aerodynamics.

3D printing

Denford CNC milling machine

The drill bit of this machine is 6.35mm. It is used to print our cars out using F1 block.
