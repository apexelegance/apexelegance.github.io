3D printing

Denford CNC milling machine

We evaluated two 3D printers: the Bambu Lab A1 Mini and the Flashforge Guider 3. Both machines were operated under identical printing parameters, and the results are as follows:
Bambu Lab A1 Mini
The printing quality was subpar, with a noticeably rough surface.
Flashforge Guider 3
In contrast, the printing quality was significantly better, featuring a smooth surface and precise structural details.
As a result, we have ultimately selected the Flashforge Guider 3 for our manufacturing needs.
